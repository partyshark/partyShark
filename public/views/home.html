How It Works

Create a party by selecting the start party button above. You will be asked to select party options and will be made an administrator to your party.
You may also join an existing party by selecting the join party button above and inputting the required party code.
For best results, create a party on the device you intent to play music from. You may change the player device once the party is created.

About

PartyShark was created as an undergraduate design project to solve a problem all of our team has experienced. When in a social setting, we often find ourselves wishing we had a collaborative music playlist sharing service powered from the cloud, easily accessable to multiple devices. This application was developed and launched strictly for educational purposes and will not be used commercially in any way.
